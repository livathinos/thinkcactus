Obligatory Hello World post

05 November 2010

This is my first post on this page. Might as well make it a short one since, this being a hello world post, it should follow a succinct and direct theme.

Welcome to my blog slash portfolio! This is a project i have started and stopped countless times in the past, since everytime i had myself convinced that this would be the time i would be able to finish it something always came up that i absolutely *had* to do, and this being a personal ego-fulfilling side-project it had to take the back seat too many a times.

But! This year was going to be different, and it is in so many ways. Proof of that fact is this very post. I actually decided that i would upload it earlier, rather than just waiting and tinkering with it until it's "perfect". So, all in all, to whoever reads this, thanks for visiting and i hope that by the time you get here i will have something a tad more useful to show for than this arbitrary first post :)

P.S.: The "work" and "contact" sections are currently under construction.
